sspvm hostel

In 1979 students and elders of Anand & V.V.Nagar get gather and decided to establish one residential hostel to encourage students of our cast by providing maximum facilities at nominal charges in heart of Vallabh Vidyanagar. With sincere efforts foundation stone was laid on 26th January 1980 (Republic Day of India) and trust is registered on 1,11,1980 at Charity commissioner.

From 1981 hostel was managed by all managing committee members tried there level best to serve students studying in hostel. All well wishers, donors and sincere hard worker by heart, by hand and by financial support also commit for up gradation of hostel.

In November 2014 Annual general meeting at Hostel appoint new managing committee in chairmanship of Shree Prafulbhai Chandegra from Vadodara, and review problems regarding hostel. With full support by all attendees decide to renovate building which requires huge repairing like masonary work, doors and windows, all water supply and sanitary works, Electrical lighting, and Painting the whole building on priority basis.

With sincere efforts of all committee members, cast brothers, well wishers work hard and renovate whole building with minimum expenses also one more building of our trust was idle since many years, renovated and utilize to serve better our trust. Kitchen is also started to provide good, nutritive food at reasonable rate govern by students.
